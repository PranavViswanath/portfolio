fundamental beliefs

principles that guide how i build AI systems.

build systems that serve people first. technology exists to solve human problems, not to showcase technical complexity.

reliability is a feature, not an afterthought. systems that work consistently under real-world conditions create lasting value.

prefer simple architectures that scale. complexity compounds over time; start simple and add complexity only when necessary.

measure outcomes, not vanity metrics. focus on metrics that directly correlate with user value and business impact.

climate-conscious AI by default. consider the environmental impact of AI systems and optimize for efficiency from day one.

write clearly; clarity compounds. clear documentation and code make systems more maintainable and accessible to others.
